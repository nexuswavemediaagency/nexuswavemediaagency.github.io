FAQ

Common questions.

What industries do you work with?

+
We have worked with schools and educational institutions, restaurants and F&B brands, retail and fashion brands, automotive showrooms, political campaigns, and service-based businesses. Our strategies adapt to each industry.

Do I need to sign a long-term contract?

+
No. Our monthly retainer services are month-to-month — we keep clients because of results, not locked contracts. Project-based work like websites and automation setups is quoted separately.

Is the ad spend included in your management fee?

+
No — ad spend goes directly to Meta or Google and is separate from our management fee. We manage your budget and campaigns; you pay the platforms directly. We recommend starting with at least PKR 15,000–30,000/month in ad spend for meaningful results.

How quickly will I see results?

+
Paid ads typically show initial results within 2 weeks as we test and optimise. Social media growth takes 2–3 months for meaningful traction. Automation results are immediate — your leads will get faster responses from day one.

Can I start with just one service?

+
Absolutely. Most clients start with social media management or paid ads, then add more services as their business grows. Everything is built to connect seamlessly as you scale.

Do you work with clients outside Pakistan?

+
Yes. We work with remote clients and communicate via WhatsApp and email. We have delivered work for clients across the globe
