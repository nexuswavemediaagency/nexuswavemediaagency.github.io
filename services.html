Frequently asked
questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Do you require long-term contracts?

+
No long-term contracts on monthly plans. We work month-to-month — we keep clients because of results, not contracts. Project-based work (websites, automation setups) is paid upfront or 50/50.

Is the ad spend included in the price?

+
No — ad spend goes directly to Meta or Google and is separate from our management fee. We recommend starting with at least $200–300/month in ad spend for meaningful results. We manage the budget and strategy.

How quickly will I see results?

+
For paid ads, you'll typically see initial results within the first 2 weeks as we test and optimise. Social media growth is slower — expect meaningful traction by month 2–3. Automation results are immediate — you'll see faster lead response from day one.

Do you work with businesses outside Pakistan?

+
Yes — we work with remote clients globally. Most of our communication happens via WhatsApp and email. We've worked with businesses in the UAE, UK, and across South Asia.

What industries do you specialise in?

+
We have experience with schools and educational institutions, retail and fashion brands, automotive showrooms, restaurants, clinics and salons, and service-based businesses. Our strategies adapt to your industry.

Can I start with just one service and add more later?

+
Absolutely. Most clients start with one service — usually social media or paid ads — and add automation and content as they grow. We build everything to work together so adding services is seamless.
